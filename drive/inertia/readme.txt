
drive-emu-check.prg:
--------------------

Checks for some/minimal modelling of r/w head inertia and retaining rotational
angle on track steps.

Expected results:
"00, OK,00,00" on real 1541/70/71
"00,EMU,00,00" on emulators

Reports SD2IEC and VICE without TDE as mass storage device and 1581 as
incompatible.

also see: https://csdb.dk/release/?id=200329
